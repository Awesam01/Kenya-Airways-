"Re-Imaging aviation in a post-covid world
Historically,air transport has doubled in size every fifteen years and has grown faster than most other industries.This projection has however been seriously disrupted by the global pandemic,that has heavily impacted and forever changed the way we travel.In light of this,the Aviation and Travel Sector increasingly needs to reimagine how it exits in order to remain sustainable in the long run.


Kenya Airways recognizes that Aviation is a forward-looking industry that stimulates development and entrepreneurship,and continously seeks tpo enhance its efficiency and effectiveness.In a post-Covid 19 world,innovation and co-creation across the entire aviation ecosystem is the catalyst for growth that will the much-needed business solutions to secure a sustainable future.We understand that the narrative around air tarvel in Africa needs to be transformed from being perceived as a luxury to a vital necessity in facilitating economic growth and development through enabling Intra-Africa trade.This can only be achieved by engaging an d co-creating with the whole eco-system to re-imagine and innovate towards prosperous Africa.


It is for this reason that Kenya Airways Fahri Innovation Hub has curated a Summit with the theme, "Re-imagining Aviation in a Post-Covid World", that seeks to bring togethr players in aviation ecosystem to kickstart the bprocess of re-imagining the future of aviation and travel in Africa,as a key enabler for economic growth and develoment.The Summit dubbed,'Africa Aviation & Travel Innovation Summit',slated vfor the 22nd-24th November 2021,will be an in-person event held at Ole Seteni Hotel,Nairobi.



Specific objectives of the Summit include;
To kickstart the process of collectively reimagining the future of Aviation and travel in Africa.
Present perpectives on practical innovation in the aviation ecosystem
Showcase KQ design challenge and kick-start the process of accelerating the winning companies.
Attract the funding for co-creating opportunities with start-ups.
Forge new value-adding partnerships
Identify emerging trends,models and technologies,disrupting the aviation and travel industry


Agenda



The summit will be an in-person event that will kick-off with a Demo-Flight and welcome event sponsored by Embraer at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, Nairobi.The Participants and speakers will then be transferred to Ole Sereni hotel for registration.


The main program will commence with opening sessions and a welcome address,followed by a keynote speech and a series of panel discussions,Therebwill be a gala dinner and networking event on the first evening. The second day nwill kick off with a panel session,followed by the KQ design challenge presented by selected entrepreneurs.The closing will be an award ceremony for the winning entrepreneur.The participants and speakers will have the option of booking a highly subsidized Safari for either 3,5 or days.The details will be provided in the registration website.
